Whole Foods with Multiple Nutrients

Why It Matters: Superfoods are nutrient-dense, meaning they deliver vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, and other health-boosting compounds in one package. Eating a variety of them helps fill nutritional gaps naturally.

Examples of Superfoods

  • Blueberries: High in Vitamin C, antioxidants, and fiber.
  • Spinach: Packed with iron, calcium, and Vitamin K.
  • Salmon: Excellent source of Vitamin D, B12, and omega-3s.
  • Sweet Potatoes: Contain Vitamin A, C, and potassium.
  • Eggs: Contain multiple B vitamins, Vitamin D, and protein.
